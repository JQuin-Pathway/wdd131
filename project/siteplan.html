4. Color Schema

The layout colors mirror a clean, reliable, and professional laundry environment:

  • Primary Color (Deep Clean Blue): #0066cc
    Usage: Applied to top structural headings (H1, H2, H3) and main navigational menus to anchor the user interface.
  • Secondary Color (Soft Sky Tint): #f0f7ff
    Usage: Applied to section backgrounds to provide visual separation and a soft, non-distracting screen canvas.
  • Accent Color (Eco Green): #2ecc71
    Usage: Applied to action items like "Book a Pickup" buttons, pricing tables highlights, and environmental badges.

5. Typography

The chosen font pairing ensures crisp readability for service instructions and operating hours:

  • Heading Font (Poppins): Sans-serif, 600/700 Weight.
    Application: Used for structural titles to give the business a strong, modern, and confident appearance.
  • Body Font (Roboto): Sans-serif, 400/500 Weight.
    Application: Used for general paragraphs, list blocks, and price lists to guarantee optimal readability across mobile devices.
