Dry Cleaning Website Site Plan (Thimbles)

Project: Professional Dry Cleaning & Laundry Service Portal

1. Site Name & Branding

Thimbles Dry Cleaning & Laundry Services

Description: A website that shows all current prices for articles of clothing.

Optional Domain Availability: ThimblesLaundryCleaning.com

2. Site Purpose

A website that shows all current prices for articles of clothing that allows you to drop pick ups/drop offs. You can also see the expected turn around dates as well as sign up for our pick up and delivery services. It will also show our tailoring hours and seamstress wait times.

3. Target Audience Scenarios

These scenario questions represent what a typical customer needs to discover upon opening the dry cleaning website:

  • Scenario 1: What is the usual turn around time per article of clothing?
  • Scenario 2: Are clothes able to be rushed to same day and how much of a price increase is that?

4. Color Schema

The layout colors are easy on the eyes and not super stand offish.

  • Primary Color (Deep Clean Blue): #0066cc
    Usage: Applied to top structural headings (H1, H2, H3) and main navigational menus to anchor the user interface.
  • Secondary Color (Soft Sky Tint): #f0f7ff
    Usage: Applied to section backgrounds to provide visual separation and a soft, non-distracting canvas.
  • Accent Color (Eco Green): #2ecc71
    Usage: Applied to action items like "Book a Pickup" buttons as well as pricing tables.

5. Typography

The chosen font pairing ensures simple readability for any customer/client who visits the website.

  • Heading Font (Poppins): Sans-serif, 600/700 Weight.
    Application: Used for structural titles to give a strong/modern appearance.
  • Body Font (Roboto): Sans-serif, 400/500 Weight.
    Application: Used for general paragraphs and price lists to guarantee optimal readability across mobile devices.
